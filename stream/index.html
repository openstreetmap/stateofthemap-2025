The main tracks of the State of the Map with most talks of the general and OSM Science programme can be watched live here. Note that if you have a “Venueless” conference ticket, you can watch all talks, ask questions and interact with other attendees on the Venueless conference platform at https://sotm2025.venueless.events/. You can enter the conference platform by clicking on the "Join online event" button on the ticket page which is linked from the emails you received from our ticketing system (pretix).
